Learning Islam


A series of textbooks teaching the fundamentals of Islam. Suitable for use at home, madrassah and school.

About Us

DPB Printers and Booksellers was established in 1996 with the objective of compiling, printing and distributing a syllabus for teaching Islam at home, the madrassah or school.

Our Books

The syllabus offers a study of Islam through its basic sources, the Qur'aan and Sunnah, its principle beliefs and practices, its laws and institutions, and their expressions.

Other Books

We have a wide variety of Islamic textbooks and resources. Visit our "Other Books" catalogue to have a look at what's available.

Contact Us

We are responsible for the compilation, printing, publishing and distribution of this series of textbooks. Also note that the textbooks can be ordered as a set or per individual grade and with or without workbooks.

About Us

DPB Printers and Booksellers was established in 1996 with the objective of compiling, printing and distributing a syllabus for teaching Islam at home, the madrassah or school. The structure and content of the series of books was compiled under the guidance of a few prominent scholars based in Cape Town, South Africa. These scholars are experts in the field of education whether at school level or at the level of a Darul-'Uloom. Although we are based in Cape Town, South Africa we will gladly distribute anywhere in the world.
